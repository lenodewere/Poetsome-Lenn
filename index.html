Poetsome Lennie

Welcome to the world of words, where emotions flow and thoughts unfold. I'm Poetsome Lennie, a poet, and wordsmith, passionate about crafting verses that touch the heart and soul. In this sacred space, I invite you to embark on a journey of self-discovery, love, and inspiration. Explore my poetry, and let the words transport you to realms both familiar and unknown.
So, sit back, breathe deeply, and let the words whisper secrets to your soul.

With every line, I share a piece of myself, hoping to resonate with your own experiences and emotions.
May my poetry be a solace, a comfort, and a reminder that you are not alone in this beautiful, messy journey called life.

Dont Wait

Don't wait till I die, kiss my bosom and lay your roses, let me gaze in life and learn to love my existence through the abundance of tender passion as a reflection from your heart to mine.

Knowing Better

I know Better Somewhere along the line I became so accustomed to being let down, that I confused to bare minimum as effort. ©Poetsome.Lennie

Just a Vibe

I will hike a mountain, To the peak
catch a sunrise
I will swim an ocean
To the dry shore,To feel warm again.
I will write a song
To relive every sunset,To meet you in a different sky.
I will find you again.
